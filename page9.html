Space Missions

Mars Exploration

Mars exploration has been a significant focus of space missions in recent years. Notable missions to Mars include:

  • Mars Rover Missions: NASA's Curiosity and Perseverance rovers have been exploring the Martian surface.
  • Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter: A satellite that studies the Martian climate and geology.
  • ExoMars: A European-Russian collaboration for studying Mars' atmosphere and searching for signs of life.

Lunar Exploration

The Moon has also been a target for space missions. Notable lunar missions include:

  • Apollo Program: NASA's series of missions in the 1960s and 1970s that landed astronauts on the Moon.
  • Chang'e Program: China's lunar exploration program, including the Chang'e 3 and Chang'e 4 missions.
  • Artemis Program: NASA's plan to return humans to the Moon, with a focus on sustainable lunar exploration.

Interplanetary Missions

Space agencies around the world have launched missions to explore other planets and celestial bodies. Examples include:

  • Voyager Probes: NASA's Voyager 1 and Voyager 2 have traveled beyond the solar system, providing data on outer planets.
  • Juno: NASA's mission to study Jupiter's composition, gravity field, and magnetic field.
  • New Horizons: A mission to Pluto and the Kuiper Belt, providing the first close-up images of Pluto.
